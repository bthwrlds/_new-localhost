About

Hello World! This is my portfolio website. It's based on my first website that I've build during my work. I hadnt had any specific tasks, so I decided to practice working with html and scss.

The idea for this website was mainly, to try out the things that I've learned so far. For presenting it as my portofolio I took some more changes. My goal was to create a website, that I would like to create for my own portofolio and that presents my sense of style and humor.

My main inspiration for webdesign are early 2010-Myspace websites. It was the first time that I got in touch with html. I mostly enjoyed working around the restrictions, MySpace was trying to set. Putting music on auto-play when loading the page and hiding the stop-button of the player was pure joy for me. (eventhough none of my friends would visit my page any more)
