About

Hello World! This is my portfolio website. It's based on my first website that I've build for myself.

The idea for this website was mainly to try out the things that I've learned so far. For presenting it as my portofolio I took some more changes. My goal was to create a website, my own portofolio which presents my sense of style and humor.

My main inspiration for webdesign are early 2010-Myspace websites. It was the first time that I got in touch with html. I mostly enjoyed working around the restrictions MySpace was trying to set: putting music on auto-play when loading the page and hiding the stop-button of the player was joy for me.

Otherwise I try to focus on simple design elements combined with playfullness. After studying more about webdesign and coding I started to like the inbuild styles, fonts and colors in html, rather than picking more specific styling.
